About us

We are an entrepreneurial team passionate about people and their stories. After extensive experiences with remote working, we have decided to create a place where people have the possibility to come together and join the Coworking and the Work-Life-Balance Community in the beatiful landscape of the Alps.
Our #WorkationAlps team is like a patchwork, we are so different but the combination makes our team really unique. Our uninqueness combined with the special atmosphere of the Alps let us offer you a plenty of possibilities: Coworking spaces with top infrastructure, Workshops in forest bathing, hiking, meditation and of course unforgettable experiences.
